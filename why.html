Why SnapPray

We built this to help everyone pray more.

Not better. Not longer. Just more.

Prayer is how we connect with God. It's how we praise Him, worship Him, ask for help, pray for others, and build the relationship that shapes everything else. Prayer can be the most important conversation in our lives.

But prayer isn't always easy.

How do I find the time? How do I start? What do I say? How do I stay consistent? What should I even be praying about?

We mean to pray when someone tells us they're struggling. We mean to pray when we see something beautiful. We mean to pray when we're scared, grateful, lost, in love. And then the moment passes — maybe we prayed, maybe we didn't.

It's not that we don't believe prayer works. It's a question of making the time, fighting off distraction, finding the words that ring true, and then connecting with God.

Meeting you where you are

Most prayer apps give you devotionals, reading plans, audio meditations — all good things, and all built for the time you've set aside to pray.

But prayer doesn't always happen at 6am with coffee. It happens in traffic. In the parking lot before a hard meeting. In the hospital waiting room. In a text message you don't know how to reply to. At midnight, when you can't sleep, and the world feels like it's ending.

You need to pray in those moments. And what do you have with you? Your phone.

That phone is already filled with the raw material of your life — contacts, photos, memories, moments. Every one of them has a prayer living inside it. SnapPray exists to help you find it.

A face you love. A feeling you can't name. A scripture that won't let you go. A friend you keep meaning to lift up. Open SnapPray, and the prayer is there — in seconds, your feelings, your memories, your life, waiting to be prayed.

What we believe

Prayer changes everything.

Prayer makes everything in life better. It's how we connect with God, how we build a relationship with Him through constant communication. God wants to hear from us.

This app doesn't pray for you. It can't.

No technology can replace a living soul saying a prayer. Words are only words. It's your faith that makes them a prayer. SnapPray is a tool — to help you find the prayer living in every photo, feeling, and moment of your life. Our prayers are short for a reason. Consider them a chance for a brief connection or the start of a longer conversation. You are the inspiration for every prayer.

Faith was never meant to be private.

We share so many things — photos, memes, texts, the things that matter to us. How you share your prayers is up to you. But you never know what a shared prayer may mean to someone. It may lift their spirits and change the trajectory of their day. It may encourage them to pray.

Prayer and technology aren't incompatible.

A printing press helped Bibles reach the world. A phone helps a missionary stay in touch with home. AI can take your direction and help you find words in moments of joy, sorrow, and everything in between — not because you can't find words yourself, but as another form of expression. SnapPray has no purpose without you. It's your prayer. Only you can pray it.

How it works

There's a prayer for every moment, and SnapPray helps you find it.

Take a photo, or pick one from your library — a face you love, a place that matters, something you can't stop looking at.

Tell SnapPray how you're feeling, whether it's anxiety, sadness, or quiet gratitude.

Pray about what's in front of you — your job, your finances, your relationships, your health.

Lift up the people you carry — family, friends, your church, your community.

Open scripture and pray the Word back to God.

Mark the milestones — a graduation, a wedding, a new baby.

Walk through the Lord's Prayer and other guided prayers when you want a deeper rhythm to your prayer life.

Whatever you bring, SnapPray helps you find the words — written in a prayer style you choose, grounded in Christian faith, inspired by your heart, and ready in seconds.

What you do with SnapPray is up to you. Save your prayers, share them. Add personal journal notes and track how they expand your prayer life and faith journey.

SnapPray belongs to you. Make it your own.
