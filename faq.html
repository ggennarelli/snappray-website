How It Works

Tap the Snap to Pray button and take a photo — or choose one from your library. SnapPray analyzes your photo using computer vision technology to understand what's in the scene.

Free users receive a curated prayer from our library, matched to the type of scene in your photo — a sunset, a family gathering, a meal, a hospital room, and more.

Premium users receive a freshly generated AI prayer, written in real time based on exactly what's in your photo — and optionally, personal context you add.

The SnapPray prayer library contains thousands of hand-crafted prayers written and reviewed by our team. They cover feelings, prayer topics, life milestones, scripture, and photo moments — in pastoral and modern styles.

Library prayers are served instantly, work offline, and are available to all users at no cost. Every prayer in the library is theologically reviewed and written to be universal — designed to work for anyone in that moment, regardless of their specific circumstances.

SnapPray's prayer rotation system ensures you see different prayers before any repeat.

Premium AI prayers are generated in real time by advanced AI. When you tap Begin My Prayer, SnapPray generates a freshly written prayer — unique to you, in that moment.

AI prayers can be personalized with your context — names, situations, specific requests. They feel different from library prayers because they're written specifically for what you shared.

Every AI prayer follows strict theological guidelines — grounded in Christian faith, never contradicting scripture, always oriented toward God's character and promises.

Library prayers are pre-written, instant, and universal. They're carefully crafted to work for anyone in a given moment — no personal details, no context needed. Available free.

AI prayers are generated fresh every time, specific to exactly what you share — your photo, your words, the names of people you're praying for. They feel personal because they are. Available on Premium.

Both are theologically sound. The difference is personalization and specificity.

The Voice Feature

SnapPray uses Apple's neural voice technology to read your prayers aloud. Natural, warm, and on-device — no internet required once set up. Available to all users, free and Premium.

For the best experience, download the Zoe neural voice:

  • Open Settings on your iPhone
  • Go to Accessibility → Spoken Content → Voices
  • Tap English
  • Find Zoe and tap the download icon

Once downloaded, Zoe works completely offline and sounds remarkably natural. The download is approximately 200MB.

Technical

AI prayer generation requires an internet connection. If your prayer didn't generate, check your connection and try again. If the issue persists, SnapPray will automatically fall back to a library prayer so you're never left without a prayer.

Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue continues, delete and reinstall SnapPray from the App Store — your Premium subscription will be restored automatically when you sign in with the same Apple ID.

If you're still having trouble, contact us at support@snappray.com.
